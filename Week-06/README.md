## 📖 Overview

During the final week of the Cyberster Blue Team Internship, I investigated a simulated insider threat scenario involving data staging, Base64 obfuscation, HTTP exfiltration, and evidence deletion. The investigation included log analysis, custom Wazuh rule development, MITRE ATT&CK mapping, and the creation of a professional incident response report based on the NIST SP 800-61 framework.
